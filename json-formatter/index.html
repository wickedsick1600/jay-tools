JSON Formatter & Validator

Paste messy JSON, clean it up, or check it for errors. Everything happens on your device — your data is never uploaded.

How to use this

  • Paste your JSON into the top box.
  • Format pretty-prints it with your chosen indent.
  • Minify strips whitespace to the smallest valid form.
  • Validate checks for errors and tells you exactly where they are.
