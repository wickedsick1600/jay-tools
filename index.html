Quick answers

Why is there no signup?
Juankit is built for speed. You open a tool, use it, leave. Accounts add friction and privacy questions we do not need for one-off tasks.
Do my files upload to your servers?
Most tools process files entirely in your browser on your device. A few features (like the prompt enhancer) call a small backend to talk to an AI provider — that path only sends what you type in that tool, not your other files.
Is Juankit tracked with ads or analytics?
No third-party analytics or ad trackers are embedded in the suite. Hosting may log normal server requests like any website.
