Prompt Enhancer

Type a rough idea. Get back a detailed, well-structured prompt you can paste straight into ChatGPT, Midjourney, Claude, or any other AI tool.

Heads up: your prompt is sent to Google Gemini to be rewritten. Don't paste anything confidential, private, or sensitive.

How this works

  • You type a rough idea — one sentence, a few words, whatever you've got.
  • Your text is sent to Google Gemini through a small server function. The AI key stays on the server — it never touches your browser.
  • You get back a polished prompt shaped for the AI tool you picked. Copy it, paste it into the other tool, done.
  • Nothing is saved. No account, no history. Each request is independent.
  • Fair-use limit. To keep the tool free for everyone, there's a cap on how often one person can hit it per hour.
