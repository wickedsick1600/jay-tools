Privacy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Short version: I don't track you. I don't collect personal data. I don't sell anything. Most of the tools don't even send your files anywhere.

What I collect on this site

This home page is just plain HTML and JavaScript. Nothing here fills out forms, saves cookies, or sends information anywhere — except for the feedback form, which I'll explain below.

When you send me feedback

If you use the feedback form on the home page, your message, and optionally your name and email (only if you fill them in), are emailed to me at devjaybusiness@gmail.com. The form is handled by Netlify (the company that hosts this site). I use this only to read what you sent and reply if you included an email. I don't add you to any mailing list.

What I don't do

  • No Google Analytics, Facebook pixels, or any tracking scripts.
  • No accounts, no logins, no passwords.
  • No newsletters or marketing emails.
  • No sale of any information to anyone, ever.

The individual tools

Each tool linked from this site has its own privacy page. Most of them (like the Image Editor and Folder Creator) process your files entirely on your own device — nothing gets uploaded. A few (like the Prompt Enhancer) need to send your text to an AI provider so it can do its job. Each tool says clearly what it does with your data. Check its privacy page before using it.

Donations

Donations go through Ko-fi or PayPal. When you click a donate button, you leave this site and land on theirs. They have their own privacy policies.

Server logs

Netlify, the service that hosts this website, automatically records standard web server logs whenever anyone visits — things like IP address, time of visit, and which page was loaded. This is normal for any website and I don't do anything special with those logs.

Questions?

Email me at devjaybusiness@gmail.com.
