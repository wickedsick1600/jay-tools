Privacy — Prompt Enhancer

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Important: Unlike the other tools on this site, this one does send your text to a third party (Google). Don't paste anything confidential, private, or sensitive into it.

What happens when you click "Enhance prompt"

  1. Your text and the AI tool you picked (like "ChatGPT" or "Midjourney") are sent to a small server function on this site.
  2. That server function passes your text to Google Gemini along with a secret AI key that stays on the server.
  3. Google Gemini sends back a polished prompt, which is returned to your browser.
  4. Nothing is stored on my side. Each request is independent — no history, no account.

What Google might do with your text

Google processes your prompt under their own rules. On the free tier, Google may use submitted content to improve their AI models. You can read the full details on Google's Gemini API terms page. Don't submit anything confidential, personal, or proprietary.

Fair-use limit

To stop one person from using up the whole daily AI quota, the server keeps a short in-memory count of how many times each IP address has called the tool. That count lives in the server's memory only and is wiped whenever the server restarts.

What I don't do

  • No accounts, no logins, no email collection.
  • No tracking scripts, no analytics, no ads.
  • No storing of your prompts or the enhanced output.

Server logs

Netlify, the company that hosts this page, automatically records standard server logs whenever anyone loads the site (IP address, time, browser type). This is normal for any website.
