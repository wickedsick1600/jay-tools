Folder Creator

Type out your folders and files below, one per line. Indent lines to put them inside another folder. Click a button to download it as a ready-to-unzip file.

Nothing is uploaded. Your text stays on your device. The .zip is built right here in your browser.

How to use this

  • Fastest way: paste your list from Excel, Word, or notes, then click Import pasted list.
  • Click "Add top-level item" to create your first folder or file.
  • Use Folder/File to choose what each row should be.
  • Use "Indent" and "Outdent" to move items under a parent folder.
  • Use "Add child" to quickly create sub-items under any folder.

Example

If you type this:

HR-Operations
  Recruitment
    Job Descriptions.docx
    Candidate Tracker.xlsx
  Onboarding
    New Hire Checklist.pdf
  Policies
    Leave Policy.docx

You'll get an HR-Operations.zip that unzips to this:

HR-Operations/
├── Recruitment/
│   ├── Job Descriptions.docx
│   └── Candidate Tracker.xlsx
├── Onboarding/
│   └── New Hire Checklist.pdf
└── Policies/
    └── Leave Policy.docx
