Folder Creator

Type out your folders and files below, one per line. Indent lines to put them inside another folder. Click a button to download it as a ready-to-unzip file.

Nothing is uploaded. Your text stays on your device. The .zip is built right here in your browser.

How to use this

  • Each line becomes one folder or file.
  • Indent with spaces or a tab to put things inside another folder. Two spaces per level is the usual choice.
  • Names with a dot and extension (like index.js or README.md) become empty files.
  • Names without an extension become folders. You can also end a name with a slash / to force it to be a folder.

Example

If you type this:

my-app
  public
    index.html
  src
    App.js
  package.json

You'll get a my-app.zip that unzips to this:

my-app/
├── public/
│   └── index.html
├── src/
│   └── App.js
└── package.json
